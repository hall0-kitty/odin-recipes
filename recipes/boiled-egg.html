Soft Boiled Eggs

What you'll need

  • 3-4 eggs
  • water
  • Saucepan
  • Slotted spoon
  • 1 Bowl of ice water

Fill the saucepan with water and bring to a rolling boil over high heat.

Reduce the heat until the water is just simmering.

Gently lower the eggs into the water one at a time

Cook for about 6-7 minutes.

Remove from the boiling water and set in the bowl of ice water. allow it to sit for about 60 seconds.
