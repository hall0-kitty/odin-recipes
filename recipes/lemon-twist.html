Lemon Twist

A classic martini - dry and smooth

What you'll need.

  • 1 stirring glass
  • 1 bar spoon
  • 60ml of plymouth gin
  • 10ml of dry vermouth
  • 1 lemon peel
  • 1 cup of clear ice

First, lets sfill the stirring glass with ice. Then we will pour in our gin and vermouth. If you dont have a bar spoon go ahead and use a skewer or a chopstick. stir the mixure as fast as you can with out causing bubbles. This should only take 30 seconds. Pour Martinini into a chilled glass. Then squeeze the peel over the drink. All that's left is to enjoy.
